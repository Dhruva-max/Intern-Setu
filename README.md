# Intern-Setu

**Bridging the Gap Between First-Generation Learners and PM Internship Opportunities**

## 🎯 Problem Statement

India's PM Internship Scheme offers thousands of opportunities, but first-generation learners with limited digital literacy struggle to navigate through the vast listings to find relevant internships that match their skills and aspirations.

## 💡 Solution

Intern-Setu is a web-based Progressive Web App (PWA) designed with a mobile-first approach to democratize access to internship opportunities. Our platform combines intelligent technology with user-friendly design to make internship discovery accessible to everyone.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🤖 Conversational AI Interview
- **2-minute profile building**: Quick and intuitive AI-powered interview process
- **Smart candidate profiling**: Captures skills, interests, and career aspirations efficiently
- **Natural language interaction**: Easy-to-use conversational interface

### 🎯 Intelligent Recommendation Engine
- **Personalized matching**: Lightweight recommendation engine suggests 3-5 best-fit internships
- **Smart filtering**: Advanced algorithms match candidates with relevant opportunities
- **Quality over quantity**: Focus on curated, high-quality recommendations

### 📱 Mobile-First Design
- **Touch-optimized interface**: Designed specifically for smartphone usage
- **Visual card-based layout**: Easy-to-scan internship cards with key information
- **Responsive design**: Seamless experience across all device sizes
- **Progressive Web App**: Works offline and provides app-like experience

### 🌐 Regional Language Support
- **Multi-language interface**: Support for English, Hindi, and Tamil
- **Localized content**: Region-specific internship listings and information
- **Cultural sensitivity**: Designed with Indian users in mind

## 🚀 Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Progressive Web App (PWA)
- **AI/ML**: Conversational AI and recommendation engine
- **Design**: Mobile-first, card-based interface
- **Languages**: English, Hindi, Tamil support
- **Platform**: Web-based with offline capabilities

## 🎯 Target Audience

- **Primary**: First-generation learners seeking internship opportunities
- **Secondary**: Students with limited digital literacy
- **Geographic Focus**: India (with regional language support)

## 📱 Getting Started

1. **Access the Platform**: Visit the web application on your smartphone
2. **Start Your Journey**: Begin the 2-minute AI interview
3. **Get Recommendations**: Receive personalized internship suggestions
4. **Apply**: Connect with relevant opportunities through our platform

## 🌟 Impact

Intern-Setu aims to:
- **Democratize access** to PM Internship Scheme opportunities
- **Reduce barriers** for first-generation learners
- **Improve matching** between candidates and internships
- **Enhance digital literacy** through intuitive design
